Purpose of PISA Analysis

├ Analysis and interpretation

└ Problem-solving beyond content recall

Domain-Level Insights

Subject Tested Through PISA Pedagogy Difference between MYP & MLC Insights Questions / Points that could be explored
Maths None MYP students scored significantly higher than MLC in Content and Mathematical Process. Do we want to understand what behavioural or other issues could have led to this significant gap when everything is the same?
English None Across all three English domains, none of the differences were statistically significant.

Both groups scored lowest in Reflecting and Evaluating. 		This looks fine in comparison. However, we might want to explore the reason and work on improving reflection and evaluating skills.
Science MYP - Traditional
MLC - PBL 		Both MLC and MYP students demonstrated broadly similar scientific literacy.

In Explaining Phenomena Scientifically, MYP showed better results. 		Do we want to explore how we can improve the explanation of scientific skills in MLC students as mostly their learning is application based? There is scope of improvement in this domain.

Overall Interpretation

Students demonstrate learning and skill growth across both models. PISA provides useful insight into application, reasoning and transfer skills, while highlighting opportunities to strengthen reflection, evaluation and explanation skills.
